Preview of Generative AI Research Tools

Listed below are 10 academic subjects chosen at random from the Research Guides offered by the Newman Library at Baruch College. The following subjects were chosen:

  • Anthropology
  • Asian Studies
  • Environmental Studies
  • Finance
  • International Business
  • Public and International Affairs
  • Information Studies
  • Company Research
  • Latinx Studies
  • History

The APIs for the following Generative AI Tools were used for this document:

  1. Chat GPT 3.5
  2. Perplexity
  3. Exa

With 10 randomly chosen subjects, Chat GPT 3.5 API was queried with the following prompt:

can you find me 10 reference questions on {academic subject}?

A document was generated with 10 questions for each of the above topics. From there, the APIs for each of the 3 Generative AI Tools was queried with every one of the 100 reference questions generated by Chat GPT 3.5.

^The Academic Subjects above link to the results.
